[[DAY]]
DAY 4 — THE RAPTOR

[[NARRATIVE]]
The light warms like nothing happened.

No sunrise. No sky shift. Just the same artificial glow turned slightly kinder, as if the system wants them calm before it takes something else.

The elevator cage rises from the ground again.

Everyone backs up automatically this time. Nobody crowds it. Nobody reaches in.

SomeDucks steps out.

He blinks hard, breath shaking, then looks at the orchard rows, the forest line, the cage frame, the faces. He understands faster than Doomfox did. Faster than Cyrus did.

He swallows. “How many.”
Rally mally answers. “Too many.”

A crate rises with SomeDucks.

More cloth. A small coil of rope. A few food packets. A sealed envelope taped to the top like it’s the real delivery.

Toad peels it off carefully and hands it to Rally mally without looking at it himself.

Rally mally opens it.

Inside is a single line.

“Schwalbe is watching....”

Rally mally reads it once, then again. His face doesn’t change, but the air around him does.

Kiwi whispers, “Who is Schwalbe.”
Lysrix answers quietly, “Someone we’re not meant to see.”
Duccarian looks at the paper like it confirms something he already suspected.
Nightmare smiles faintly, almost pleased. “Good. An audience.”

Rally mally folds the note and puts it away. “We don’t chase ghosts. We work.”

They feed SomeDucks, brief him, give him a place near the shelter line. Cyrus stays close but says little. The orchard sector is already starting to look lived in with stacked stones, vine cordage, and the cage standing like a warning nobody wants to need.

Then GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) gets worse.

He has been hungry all morning. Not normal hungry. Hollow hungry. He eats his ration, then watches others eat like his body is counting calories it never got.

space fan checks his skin and flinches. It looks tight in patches, irritated like it’s peeling under the surface.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) whispers, “I feel like my bones are rearranging.”
janhk. looks at him and says, “That’s not a sentence people say and stay normal.”
Ut tightens his grip on the rope coil without meaning to.
Cyrus watches the cage frame.
SomeDucks watches GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) with wide eyes, then looks away, scared of what he might see.

Rally mally makes the call before panic makes it for him. “We keep him in sight. We don’t crowd him. We keep the cage ready.”

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) tries to stand and his posture is wrong. His back is too stiff, shoulders rolling oddly as if joints are shifting. He takes one step and winces.

Then the convulsions start again.

He drops to his knees, shaking hard enough to rattle his teeth. His breathing comes in fast bursts, like he’s sprinting while trapped in place.

space fan kneels immediately. “GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast). Look at me. Stay with me.”
Lysrix crouches opposite, voice steady. “Don’t hold him down. Just keep him from hitting his head.”
Kiwi hovers a step behind, eyes fixed on the elevator zone and the pale strip near it, like he’s trying to connect invisible dots.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) gasps, claws at the grass, and then freezes.

For a long second, nothing moves.

Then he sheds.

Not like a snake. Like a system tearing away something it no longer needs.

Skin flakes come loose in dry sheets. His hands tremble and his nails harden into dark points. His spine arches sharply, and a tail pushes out beneath his clothes, tearing fabric with a slow brutal certainty.

SomeDucks stumbles backward. “What is that.”
Cyrus says nothing. He just grips the rope coil harder.
Ut says, voice flat, “Stay back.”
Nightmare’s eyes brighten like he’s watching a weapon being born.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) rises.

His shape is no longer human. A raptor form, low and powerful, muscle packed tight, balance perfect. His breathing is louder now, his head tilting as if he’s hearing in wider ranges.

He looks around.

Not hostile.

Confused.

Hungry.

He takes one step toward the orchard line, then another, claws digging lightly into dirt.

Rally mally’s voice remains level even as his hands shake. “Cage. Now. Slowly.”

They move like a drill they never practiced.

Ut and Cyrus take the left rope line.
Toad and Rally mally take the right.
janhk. guides the door mechanism with careful hands.
space fan stays closest, talking softly like she’s guiding a scared animal. “Easy. Easy. You’re okay. You’re still you.”
Lysrix stands slightly behind space fan, eyes locked on the raptor’s posture.
Kiwi watches the tail, the claws, the way it shifts weight.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) backs up instinctively, startled by the ropes.

SomeDucks tries to help close the cage door from the side.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) flinches, tail swinging without intent.

A claw catches SomeDucks across the forearm, shallow but sharp.

SomeDucks yelps and jerks back, blood beading fast.

space fan snaps, “Hold still.”
Lysrix says, “Light injury. Clean it.”
Rally mally says, “Door. Close it.”
Kiwi whispers, “He didn’t mean it.”

The cage door locks.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) presses his head against the bars once, then pulls back, breathing hard. He looks at the group as if he expects them to hate him.

They don’t move closer.

They don’t move away.

They just watch.

After a minute, the raptor lowers itself to the ground and starts scratching the dirt with a claw.

Slow, deliberate lines.

Not random.

Letters, crude but intentional.

He writes a single word.

DAVE

space fan’s eyes sting. “He remembers.”
Ninnginni whispers, “Of course he does.”
Ut exhales slowly, shoulders dropping a fraction. “He’s not gone.”
janhk. stares at the word like it’s proof the Maze can’t fully steal a person.
Cyrus finally speaks, voice rough. “He’s still in there.”
SomeDucks holds his bleeding arm and nods, swallowing fear. “I believe it.”

Rally mally steps closer to the cage, just one step, careful. “GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast). If you understand me, blink.”

The raptor blinks once.

Then again.

Rally mally looks back at the group. “We keep him fed. We keep him safe. We keep us safe.”

Nightmare’s smile fades into something unreadable. “Or we keep a monster in a box.”
Lysrix answers without looking at him, voice cold and calm. “We keep a friend alive.”

SomeDucks sits down with his back against a tree, clutching his bandaged arm, shaking.

The note in Rally mally’s pocket feels heavier than paper now.

Schwalbe is watching.

And the day ends with the Peeps understanding a new truth.

Aurora’s Rest can grow.

The Maze can change.

And now they can too.

C A R L is out there somewhere.

And if C A R L can turn a person into a raptor, then this place can turn anything into anything.

[[NIGHT]]
NIGHT 4 THE EXILE

The light cools and the world holds its breath.

Aurora’s Rest is bigger now, but the size only makes the dark feel wider. The orchard rows stand like silent fences. The dense forest looks like a black wall of needles and trunks. The cage sits in the middle of it all like a new law.

Rally mally calls everyone close. “Nobody wanders. Nobody goes near the Maze line. We listen first.”

The shifting starts.

Not the gentle grind from earlier nights. This is heavier, slower, like the Maze is dragging whole sections into new positions. The sound crawls through the soil and up into their teeth.

janhk. presses a palm to the ground, eyes tight. “It’s moving more than yesterday.”
Ut resets a boundary stone that vibrated out of place and plants it harder. “It wants us to step wrong.”
Kiwi watches the dark corridor gaps and whispers, “It wants someone to run.”

SomeDucks sits with his bandaged arm tucked in close, trying not to show how much the scratch shook him. “If that thing could do that by accident…”
space fan answers softly, “Then we don’t crowd him. We don’t force him. We stay smart.”

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) shifts inside the cage, breathing loud. His tail taps the dirt once, restless, then stops. He scratches the ground with a claw again, slower tonight, like writing is hard without hands.

He writes two short marks that look like a name trying to form and failing.

Lysrix crouches near the cage but keeps a safe distance. “He’s still thinking.”
Ninnginni whispers, “He’s still here.”
Duccarian stands with arms loose, studying the raptor like a puzzle. “Or he is a different kind of here.”

Nightmare doesn’t look at the cage much. He’s watching Axlot.

Axlot has been wrong all day, and tonight it gets worse.

He paces the edge of the camp circle, too fast, breathing too hard, eyes snapping from face to face as if everyone is hiding something from him. His skin looks damp even in the cool. His jaw keeps working like he’s grinding teeth down.

Axlot points at the dark orchard rows. “I heard it.”
Rally mally keeps his voice level. “You heard the Maze.”
Axlot shakes his head, sharp and angry. “No. Something else. Something crawling.”

Doomfox stays near Cyrus, both of them quiet, both of them looking like they regret ever stepping out of the elevator.
Cyrus murmurs, “He’s going to break.”
Doomfox answers, “He already did.”

Toad shifts his grip on an axe handle and keeps it low, not threatening, just ready. “Nobody swings unless Rally mally says so.”
pinkfml whispers from under Dave the Tree, “I hate the way he’s looking at us.”

Axlot stops pacing.

He goes still like a switch flipped.

Then his body jerks once, hard, and he drops to one knee.

Rally mally steps forward. “Axlot. Stay with me.”
Ut moves in to help.
Axlot lashes out with his arm without thinking.

Ut catches the motion and backs off fast. “No. No contact.”

Axlot’s breathing turns into a rasp. His shoulders widen in small ugly increments, like muscle is being packed in too quickly. His spine arches, and his posture changes from human upright to something that wants to be lower to the ground.

His hands stiffen.

His fingers look wrong, longer, thicker, nails darkening.

He makes a sound that is not a word.

Kiwi takes a step back on instinct.
SomeDucks stumbles too, keeping his injured arm tucked away.
space fan raises both hands, palms open, voice shaking but clear. “Axlot. Listen. We’re not attacking you.”

Axlot’s head snaps toward her and his eyes look empty, like the person inside stepped away.

He crawls forward.

Fast.

Low to the ground.

Not human movement anymore.

Rally mally lifts his voice, firm. “Everyone back. Do not corner him.”

Nightmare smiles faintly, almost pleased to be right about something.
Duccarian watches with cold focus. “That is not panic. That is conversion.”

Axlot lunges at the nearest figure, not to kill, just to smash and dominate.

Toad shoves a crate between them like a shield and takes the hit on the wood instead of a body. The crate cracks. The sound is loud in the night.

That loud sound answers back from the Maze.

A distant shriek.

Not a human scream.

A hunting call.

Then another, closer.

Then a chorus of them, echoing through corridors and over walls.

janhk. whispers, “They’re in the Maze.”
Ut says, “They’re moving.”
Kiwi breathes, “They’re coming.”

Rally mally makes the decision with no hesitation. “We don’t fight him here. We can’t risk the camp. We exile him.”

Cyrus looks shocked. “Exile where.”
Rally mally doesn’t blink. “To the Maze edge. To whatever is answering those screams.”

space fan swallows hard. “That’s death.”
Rally mally nods. “I know.”

Lysrix’s face tightens, but she doesn’t argue. “If he stays, he kills us first.”
Nightmare finally speaks, voice calm. “This is what the system wanted.”

They move as a group, not rushing, not surrounding Axlot, guiding him the only way you can guide something that no longer understands language.

Ut and janhk. keep wide angles, moving boundary stones aside just enough to open a path.
Kiwi stays close to Rally mally, ready to yank him back if Axlot snaps.
Toad keeps the axe low but present.
Cyrus and Doomfox carry a coil of rope between them, not to bind, just to steer distance if needed.
SomeDucks watches the dark orchard rows, shaking.
pinkfml doesn’t move much, but her eyes track every motion, tears caught and not falling.

Ninnginni stays near the cage and looks back once. “GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) is watching.”
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) presses his head close to the bars, breathing hard, and scratches one rough line in the dirt.

A warning shape. A claw mark that looks like a stop sign without words.

They reach the Maze threshold.

The air is colder there. The sound gets swallowed again. The corridor beyond is open just enough to feel like a mouth waiting.

Axlot crawls to the edge and pauses, head tilting as if he can hear better in the dark. Then he makes that inhuman sound again, and it carries into the corridors like a beacon.

The answering shrieks come closer.

Now they can hear the mechanical rasp inside the sound, like metal joints moving too fast.

Quiggs.

They don’t see them clearly, but they hear them.

A raptor body shape in the dark, heavier and wider than GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast), moving low and fast. A scorpion tail that clicks and scrapes on stone. Arms too long, built for climbing walls, for crawling where people can’t follow.

They are fast, but the metal makes them loud.

They swarm the Maze at night like patrols.

Rally mally backs everyone up slowly. “Do not run. Do not chase. We leave him.”

Axlot crawls into the corridor on his own.

No goodbye.

No last words.

Just a body that used to be a person disappearing into the Maze.

The first Quigg scream cuts close, sharp enough to make pinkfml flinch hard.
SomeDucks clamps a hand over his mouth.
Doomfox whispers, “We can’t help him.”
Cyrus answers, “If we try, we join him.”

They retreat to Aurora’s Rest, but the sound follows.

The Quiggs shrieks rise and fall, echoing through the corridors like the Maze is amplifying them. Then a heavier noise joins the chaos, a deeper presence that makes the smaller calls shift into something like obedience.

C A R L.

They do not see C A R L.

They only feel the change when the night itself seems to tense, like the world is bracing around a single moving mass.

The Peeps can hear Axlot out there.

Not words.

Just frantic movement, scraping stone, a burst of struggling sound that ends too quickly.

Then the Quiggs calls turn into a feeding chorus.

SFW and distant, but unmistakable.

Rally mally closes his eyes once, hard. “We don’t listen.”
Lysrix whispers, “We can’t not.”
Kiwi grips the boundary stone until his knuckles whiten.
Toad turns away and stares at the orchard rows like he’s trying to delete the sound by looking elsewhere.
Ninnginni sits down in the dirt and shakes silently.
space fan crouches by Dave the Tree and breathes through her nose like she’s holding herself together with technique.
pinkfml starts crying without sound, shoulders trembling.
Duccarian stands rigid, face unreadable, like he refuses to give the Maze the satisfaction of seeing him react.
Nightmare watches the dark corridor and looks almost impressed.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) scratches the ground again inside the cage, faster now, the claw lines harsh.

He writes a single word in uneven letters.

NO

Then the night goes still for one heartbeat.

A pause so perfect it feels staged.

And from deep inside the Maze, C A R L roars.

It is not a normal roar. It is a pressure wave.

The ground shakes. The orchard trees tremble. The cage bars hum. Dust falls from seams they did not know existed.

Every Peep feels it in their chest, like the sound reached inside and grabbed.

Rally mally stands up straight, voice low and final. “Back to shelters. Now. No lights. No noise.”

They move.

Quiet.

Small.

Because tonight taught them a new rule without writing it down.

Quiggs can be escaped.

C A R L cannot.
