[[DAY]]
DAY 3 — THE SECOND LEVER

[[NARRATIVE]]
Day 3 arrives without ceremony.

The light warms again like nothing happened, like the Maze didn’t grind all night and like GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) didn’t convulse in the dirt while everyone tried not to panic.

Nobody feels rested. They just feel awake.

Rally mally does headcount first. He forces the words out like a ritual. “Say your name when I call it.”

Axlot answers too fast, like he’s daring someone to accuse him.
Nightmare answers without looking up.
Duccarian answers calmly.
-MAD$- answers while already sorting ration scraps.
space fan answers while checking GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast)’s face for fever.
Lysrix answers quietly, eyes flicking to the elevator zone.
Kiwi answers and keeps his gaze away from the pale strip.
Ut answers and steps toward the boundary stones immediately.
janhk. answers and starts writing notches into the soil again.
Ninnginni answers with a weak joke voice that doesn’t fully return.
pinkfml answers in a whisper, still pale.
Doomfox answers like he’s surprised he’s still here.
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) answers last, voice hoarse, and then looks down at his ankle like it betrayed him.

Rally mally makes the first rule of the day. “No one approaches the elevator zone alone. Not even for a second.”

Axlot opens his mouth.
Lysrix beats him to it. “Agreed.”
Kiwi nods once, too quickly.
Nightmare smiles faintly like agreement is a tool he can use later.

They eat the last of the nutrient bars. Not enough. Never enough.

-MAD$- hands out portions without argument. “We’re not collapsing today. Eat.”
space fan keeps half a water packet aside and holds it to GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast)’s lips. “Small sips. Don’t gulp.”
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) tries to joke and fails. “Dave the Tree is judging me.”
Ninnginni gives him a real smile this time. “Dave judges all of us.”

The forest hectare they unlocked yesterday sits there, dense and silent, still no animals, still no life noises. It looks like a picture someone painted and forgot to finish.

Ut comes back from the tree line with two handfuls of dry branches and a report. “No tracks. No droppings. No insects. That’s unnatural.”
janhk. adds from the seam line, “It’s a constructed biome. It’s there to be harvested.”
Duccarian murmurs, “Or to hide things.”
Kiwi says, “Or both.”

Rally mally doesn’t let them spiral. “Today we unlock another zone. We need more space. More material. And we need to know where the next lever is.”

Nightmare steps forward like he’s been waiting for permission. “Then we go.”
Rally mally shakes his head. “Three people. Fast. Quiet. Smart.”
Axlot laughs. “You mean not me.”
Rally mally meets his eyes. “Not you.”

Axlot’s jaw tightens, but he doesn’t argue this time. He just looks away like he’s holding a grudge in his teeth.

Rally mally points to the team. “Ut. Kiwi. janhk.”
janhk. pauses mid-scratch. “Me.”
Rally mally answers, “Yes. You read seams. You spot tracks. You’re going.”
Kiwi swallows. “Okay.”
Ut nods once. “In and out.”

space fan steps forward. “If you find anything that stings, snaps, or clicks, you don’t touch it. You come back.”
Lysrix adds quietly, “And if the lights change, you freeze.”
Duccarian says, “If the Maze wants you dead, it won’t announce it.”
Ninnginni mutters, “Great pep talk.”

They go.

The Maze entrance is open wider today, like it expects them now. Like the system rewarded yesterday’s lever pull with a deeper corridor.

The air inside is colder than Aurora’s Rest. Sound gets swallowed. Even footsteps feel wrong, like the floor is damp stone pretending to be dry.

Ut leads. Kiwi watches corners. janhk. watches seams.

They move slowly at first, then faster as confidence returns.

They pass one pressure plate that Ut spots in time. He points, and Kiwi steps around it without speaking.

janhk. crouches, studies the mechanism. “Spring housing. Needle slot. Similar to last one.”
Kiwi’s throat tightens. “So there are more.”
Ut says, “We treat every stone like it wants blood.”

They follow the corridor deeper than yesterday, guided by nothing except instinct and the idea that the lever has to exist somewhere.

Then they find it.

A larger alcove, a metal handle bolted into stone, thick enough to require weight and will.

Ut checks the floor first. Nothing obvious.

Kiwi checks the wall edges. No seam traps.

janhk. checks the lever mount itself. “It’s reinforced. It expects force.”
Ut braces. “On three.”
Kiwi places his hands too.
janhk. stacks in.

They pull.

The lever doesn’t move.

They pull again.

It gives with a brutal clang and slams into place like the Maze just accepted a payment.

An alarm detonates through the walls.

Not a siren. A clean tone, like a system confirmation.

Back in Aurora’s Rest, the Peeps freeze at the sound.

Nightmare’s head snaps up. “That’s the lever.”
Rally mally says, “Hold positions.”
Lysrix watches the invisible boundary line like it might peel away.

It does.

The smog barrier on another side of Aurora’s Rest thins, then collapses like fog being vacuumed.

A new hectare reveals itself.

Not forest this time.

A grid of orchard-like trees in perfect rows, spaced too evenly, trunks identical thickness, branches pruned into the same shapes. Between rows, the ground is bare dirt with faint lines—like it was already meant for planting.

Doomfox whispers, “That’s… farmland.”
-MAD$- breathes out hard. “That’s survival.”
space fan says, “That’s hope.”
Duccarian murmurs, “That’s expectation.”
Lysrix says quietly, “That’s a cage that gets bigger.”

The Maze team returns.

Ut comes in first, hand up, signaling no chase.
Kiwi follows, eyes wide.
janhk. comes last, breathing hard, but smiling for the first time since waking up.

Rally mally meets them at the boundary. “Report.”
Ut answers, “Lever two. More traps deeper in.”
Kiwi adds, “Pressure plates. Needle housings. We can spot them if we slow down.”
janhk. says, “The Maze is designed. That means we can learn it.”

Rally mally nods. “Good. We expand camp toward the orchard zone. We build better shelter. We plant.”
-MAD$- is already moving. “Axes to the forest. Hoe to the orchard. Seeds get inventoried.”
Ninnginni exhales. “We’re farming in a nightmare. Cool.”

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) stands up too fast and sways. space fan catches him.
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) mutters, “I’m fine.”
Lysrix watches him carefully. “No you’re not.”
Kiwi looks at GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast)’s ankle and then looks away like the thought hurts.

Axlot watches the lever team with narrowed eyes, anger mixing with something like fear. “So they trust you now.”
Ut doesn’t respond.
janhk. doesn’t respond.
Kiwi doesn’t respond.

Rally mally does. “We trust competence. Earn it.”
Axlot’s face tightens. “I did earn it.”
Rally mally’s voice is flat. “Not lately.”

Nightmare laughs once, quiet. “Good. The group fractures. That’s always fun.”

Duccarian looks toward the Maze corridor, calm as ever. “Two zones unlocked. Two remain.”
Lysrix answers, “And the Maze already took payment.”
space fan whispers, looking at the orchard rows, “We’re growing food under a sky that isn’t real.”

Rally mally raises his voice just enough to keep everyone in one story. “We build. We plant. We rest when we can. And tonight we watch GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast). And we watch Axlot.”

No one argues.

Because the day feels like progress.

And progress in this place always means the system is preparing something else.

[[NIGHT]]
NIGHT 3 THE NEW PEET AND THE CAGE

The light cools again, and everyone feels it like a bruise.

Aurora’s Rest is bigger now. Two extra hectares unlocked means more space, more trees, more ground to pretend they own. But the size only makes the shadows feel wider. The Maze doesn’t need to be close to feel present.

Rally mally gathers everyone near the boundary stones and speaks low. “Same rules. Pairs. No one crosses lines alone. No one approaches the elevator zone. And we build a real cage tonight.”

Axlot laughs without humor. “For me.”
Rally mally doesn’t look away. “For anything that changes.”

Kiwi’s eyes flick to GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast).
Lysrix’s eyes do too.
space fan keeps her hands busy, checking GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast)’s temperature with the back of her fingers like she’s trying to prove calm is possible.

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) looks better and worse at the same time. His breathing is steadier, but his skin looks tight, irritated in patches. He keeps scratching his forearm like something under it wants out.

Ninnginni watches him and whispers, “Are we sure he’s okay.”
Duccarian answers quietly, “Okay is a temporary state.”
janhk. says, “He’s stable. For now.”
Ut adds, “For now is all we ever get.”

The Maze shifts begin earlier tonight. The grinding comes in long slow waves, stone dragging against stone beyond the walls. The orchard trees don’t move with wind, but they creak anyway, like the sound is traveling through the ground into their trunks.

pinkfml flinches every time the grinding peaks. She sits under one of the sad trees with her knees up and tries to make herself small.
space fan sits near her, not touching, just present. “If you need to talk, talk.”
pinkfml whispers, “If I talk, it hears me.”
space fan answers, “Then whisper at me. I’ll be louder later if we need it.”

Rally mally points people into tasks.

-MAD$- manages rationing and tool control. “Axes stay with me until morning. Nobody steals tools in the dark.”
Ut checks perimeter stones and resets any that shifted from foot traffic.
janhk. carves a clearer warning into a wood plank from the orchard and plants it near the elevator zone. DO NOT APPROACH.
Ninnginni and GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) weave vines into thicker cords until GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast)’s hands start shaking again and he has to stop.
Nightmare stands watch, still and hungry-eyed, like he enjoys that everyone is afraid.
Duccarian watches the group’s movement patterns like he’s memorizing who stands near who.

Axlot paces again.

Not as wild as Night 2, but wrong in a different way. He keeps looking at people’s hands, people’s bags, the elevator, the boundary stones. His jaw works like he’s chewing words he won’t say.

Lysrix walks up to Rally mally quietly. “If he spikes tonight, we need a plan.”
Rally mally nods. “We restrain. We don’t kill.”
Nightmare hears and smiles faintly. “Soft.”
Rally mally answers, “Human.”

The elevator cable settles with a metallic sigh.

Everyone freezes.

It isn’t loud. That’s what makes it terrifying. A sound that means something moved without permission.

Kiwi steps closer to Rally mally, voice low. “It’s rising.”
Rally mally raises his hand. “Nobody rushes it. Nobody touches it.”

The elevator cage rises from the ground like a slow breath.

And when it stops, someone is inside.

Cyrus steps out carefully, blinking hard as if he’s been woken mid-fall. His clothes are dusted with metal grit. He looks at the orchard rows, the forest line, the faces, and his expression shifts from confusion to instant comprehension.

Cyrus speaks first, voice tight. “This is a Maze.”
Ut answers, “Yes.”
janhk. adds, “And it moves at night.”
Cyrus swallows. “Of course it does.”

Supplies rise with him. Two crates. Heavier than Doomfox’s day. Cleanly labeled.

Rope. More cloth. A small saw. A simple hammer. A bag of nails. Another packet of seeds. A bit more food than yesterday, like the system approves of progress.

-MAD$- is already sorting. “Tools to the center. Food gets counted. Rope goes to the cage.”
Ninnginni mutters, “It’s like we’re being coached.”
Duccarian replies, “We are.”

Cyrus watches the elevator mouth like it’s a predator. “People died in that.”
Rally mally answers flatly, “Yes.”
Cyrus nods once, stiff. “Then I’m not going near it.”
Kiwi whispers, “Good.”
Lysrix watches Cyrus carefully. “Do you remember anything before you woke in the cage.”
Cyrus shakes his head. “Nothing. Just moving. And metal.”

They get to work immediately. The night is never truly quiet here, so they build faster than they should.

Ut and janhk. lead structure. They choose a spot between the orchard and the forest, far from the elevator zone and far from the boundary stones. Solid ground. Open sight lines.

Rally mally assigns muscle and hands.

Axlot is told to cut wood and stay in the lighted camp area. “You don’t leave the circle.”
Axlot bristles but obeys, swinging the axe too hard, too fast, as if chopping the tree is chopping the world.
Nightmare watches Axlot swing. “He’s going to break eventually.”
Duccarian replies, “Everyone breaks. The system just chooses the timing.”

space fan keeps near GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast), offering water and forcing him to sit when his hands shake. “You don’t get to be brave by collapsing.”
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) tries to grin. “Dave would be disappointed.”
space fan smiles, small. “Dave can cope.”

Lysrix helps with knots. Her fingers move fast. Clean. Controlled. She doesn’t look at the elevator once.

Kiwi keeps listening to the ground for the shifting rhythm. He points out a vibration wave and Rally mally uses it to time quiet moments. They learn to build between grind peaks so they can hear each other.

By the time the Maze movement slows, a cage frame stands. Rough. Ugly. Real.

Rally mally tests it with his weight. It holds.

He looks at everyone and says the hard part. “This is not punishment. This is safety. If someone changes, we protect the group. And we protect them.”

Axlot laughs once. “So if I change, you lock me up.”
Rally mally nods. “If you become dangerous. Yes.”
Axlot’s eyes flash. “I’m already dangerous.”
Lysrix answers quietly, “That’s why it’s a rule.”

The Maze shifting finally stops.

Silence returns like a trap.

Cyrus sits down for the first time and exhales like he’s been holding his breath since he rose out of the elevator. “So this is Night 3.”
Ninnginni answers, “We stopped counting nights like normal people.”
Cyrus nods slowly. “Good. Normal got us nowhere.”

GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) shivers once and scratches his forearm until space fan catches his wrist gently.
space fan whispers, “Easy.”
GalaxyTwentea(dino enthusiast) nods, eyes unfocused. “I feel weird.”
Kiwi hears it and stiffens.
Lysrix hears it and looks away too fast.

Rally mally does final watch assignments.

Nightmare takes first watch. Ut takes second. janhk. takes third. KiWi takes early morning. Rally mally refuses to sleep fully and sits with his back to a post, eyes half open.

No one says good night.

Not here.

Because tonight didn’t feel like survival.

It felt like preparation.

And everyone can feel it.

Day 4 is coming.
